Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how ASTECH ("we", "us", or "our") handles data in the SysMon Android mobile application (the "App").

1. Audio Data & Visualization (RECORD_AUDIO)

Strict Privacy Guarantee: The App requires the RECORD_AUDIO permission solely to provide real-time audio visualization features.

  • No Recording: We do not record or save any audio data.
  • No Storage: No audio files are ever created or stored on your device.
  • No Transmission: Audio data is never transmitted over the internet or shared with third parties.

All audio processing occurs locally on your device in volatile memory and is discarded immediately after visualization.

2. System Monitoring Data

SysMon Android monitors system telemetry such as CPU load, RAM usage, Network speeds, and Battery status. This data is used exclusively to display real-time statistics to you within the App and the overlay widget. This information is never collected or sent to our servers.

3. Permissions Usage

  • Internet & Network State: Used to monitor upload/download speeds and to handle in-app purchases.
  • System Alert Window: Used to display the floating monitor widget over other applications.
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep the monitor active while the App is in the background.
  • Post Notifications: Used to show live status in the notification tray.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Play Services for core functionality and Google Play Billing for "Pro" features. These services may collect information used to identify you in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We do not collect personal information. Any settings or "Presets" you create are stored locally on your device and are deleted if you uninstall the App.
